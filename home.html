Welcome to the USAFA Texbook Exchange!

This project is a cadet innitiative to help others save money and reduce waste in the cadet wing.

 

 

Buy

In the buy tab, you can browse for various textbooks being sold by other cadets. You can view books based on class, subject, price, and dorm location

 

 

Sell

In the sell tab, you can list your used textbooks for sale. You can determine your own price and choose how you would like to be contacted.

 

 

Account

In the account tab, you can edit your account information including contact and lcocation information
